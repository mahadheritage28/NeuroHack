How AuraMed Works

AuraMed is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to address challenges in underserved communities. Follow these simple steps to access healthcare with ease.

1

Sign Up & Set Up Your Profile

Create your account and fill in your health details. This step helps us provide tailored services, including AI-powered symptom checks and recommendations.

2

Explore Features

Access tools like the bed availability tracker, AI-driven symptom checker, nearby hospital locator, and inventory updates to make informed decisions about your care.

3

Book & Manage Services

Schedule appointments, request teleconsultations via video call, or book tests with ease. Keep track of your prescriptions and medical history securely.

4

Receive Seamless Care

Get real-time updates, emergency alerts, and personalized care from nearby hospitals. Whether in-person or virtual, AuraMed ensures a smooth healthcare journey.
